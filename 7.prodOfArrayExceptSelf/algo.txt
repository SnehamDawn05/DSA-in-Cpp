https://leetcode.com/problems/product-of-array-except-self/

Initialize two arrays:

pProd[i] = product of all elements up to index i.

sProd[i] = product of all elements from index i to end.

Fill pProd from left → right, sProd from right → left.

For each index i:

Result = pProd[i-1] * sProd[i+1].

Handle edges separately (first = sProd[1], last = pProd[n-2]).

Return result array.
